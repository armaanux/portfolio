About me

Creative, detail-focused and always curious.

  1. 01

    01 —What I do

    Over the last four years, I've worked on SaaS platforms, mobile apps, dashboards, websites, and product redesigns for startups and online businesses across different industries worldwide.

    I focus on creating clean, modern, and easy-to-use experiences with strong attention to layout, responsiveness, and visual clarity.

  2. 02

    02 —How I work

    I naturally pay attention to small details like spacing, typography, interactions, and overall balance. I care a lot about making products feel smooth, intuitive, and visually refined without making them feel overloaded.

    Most of my process is about simplifying experiences and refining things until they feel effortless to use.

  3. 03

    03 —Outside work

    Outside design, I spend a lot of time training at the gym and fitness has become a big part of my routine. I'm also really into Formula 1 and follow every race weekend closely.

    When I'm not working, I'm usually listening to music, playing Valorant, or planning trips that rarely happen.
